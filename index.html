About Me

In 2018, I took a leap of faith and officially immersed myself in the tech world. I attended countless startup and coding meetups, networking with so many amazing people. I even got to experience Startup Bus in 2018, where I was the only developer on my team and had to build a golf pairing application. After completing a full-time, in-person, full-stack React/Ruby developer program with Suncoast Developer's Guild in 2019, my Software QA journey began shortly afterward.

I was brought on by Sourcetoad to spearhead their SQA initiative and discovered a genuine passion for software quality assurance. I excelled in rapidly adapting to various projects, and uncovered meaningful bugs and defects. The dynamic and analytical nature of SQA captivated me, knowing that my efforts contributed to creating frustration-free software for a positive user experience. With a foundation in full-stack web development, I've been able to quickly pick up Cypress.io, Jest, and Selenium, which has continued to further my love for what I do.

Outside the professional realm, I indulge my continuing curiosity by working on apps, and exploring diverse topics from SDLC models to UI design.

If you're seeking someone who combines a background in development with a passion for SQA, I would be a great addition to your team or company. I thrive in collaborative environments with a culture of excellent communication, supportive management and a commitment to continued growth.
